AI & Autonomous Humanoid Robot Development Workshop

During the workshop, participants are introduced to the basic of Robot Operating System in Nvidia Jetson Nano and learn how to control Robotis Dynamixel MX-28 servo motor via OpenCR as controller board and Nvidia Jetson Nano as the Single Board Computer (SBC).

Besides, basic AI introduction such as Deep Learning (DL) and Fuzzy Logic (FL) was exposed to the participants to develop the intelligent vision system for the robot in completing human tasks. My biggest challenge so far with this training is that I had to prepare a detailed public speaking for the participants. I was really anxious yet delighted to have this chance. Fortunately, with good preparation and a little luck, the training was a success.

To end this post, I would like to show my gratitude to all the lecturers from my previous polytechnic for trusting me and giving me this opportunity to become the trainer and technical speaker for this event. Hopefully, in the future, I can get more chances and opportunities to share my knowledge with others, and at the same time refine my technical skill and public speaking.
